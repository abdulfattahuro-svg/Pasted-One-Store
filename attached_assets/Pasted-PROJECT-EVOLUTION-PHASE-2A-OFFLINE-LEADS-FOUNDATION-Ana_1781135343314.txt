PROJECT EVOLUTION PHASE 2A: OFFLINE LEADS FOUNDATION

Analyze the existing codebase before making changes.

The platform now supports:

* Products
* Affiliates
* Product-specific referral links
* Product attribution
* Product analytics

Next we need to support offline affiliate marketing.

GOAL

Allow affiliates to submit leads manually for products that are sold offline.

Examples:

* Solar installation
* Real estate
* Insurance
* Consulting
* Agency services
* MLM products
* Training programs

A lead should be trackable from submission through final conversion.

DO NOT build the CRM pipeline yet.

Build the lead foundation first.

* affiliateId
* affiliateCode
* productSlug
* fullName
* phone
* email
* notes
* status
* source
* createdAt
* updatedAt

Status values:

* new
* contacted
* interested
* won
* lost

Source values:

* affiliate_submission
* website_form

* import
